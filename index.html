About Us

It is our pleasure to introduce ourself as one of the leading textile grey supplier and commission agent in Delhi-NCR that has been in business for past 25 years.

We have been an established and popular company with an excellent track record for the best customer satisfaction. We have never compromised on the quality and the services provided to the customers. We believe in keeping the customers happy and providing them with the products at a very competent price.

We deal in all types of cotton and rayon fabrics (plain and crape) and modal. We deal in cambric, voile, poplin, sheeting, drill, twill, duck, lycra, etc. We deal in all types of counts which include 7s, 10s, 20s, 30s, 40s, 50s, 60s, 80s, etc. and width varying from 36” to 142”. We can provide all types of grey fabrics which are weaved on airjet, sulzer, ruti, powerloom, etc.

We have our supply from Erode, Tirupur, Somanur, Palladam, Madurai, Dindigul, Bhiwandi, Ichalkaranji, Ujjain, Kishangarh, Balotra, etc.

We look forward to a wonderful relationship together and success for all concerned.
